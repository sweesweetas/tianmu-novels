The Mind Behind the Stories
甜慕
Tián Mù — Sweet Longing
"I don't write stories. I open a door — and whatever has been collecting in the dark comes through."

甜慕 is a pen name for a mind that refuses to stay quiet. These pages are where the overflow goes — the characters who wouldn't leave, the worlds that arrived without invitation, the feelings too strange and too specific to fit anywhere else.

There is no grand method here. No outline, no blueprint. Only imagination, unfiltered and unashamed, poured out until the container is empty — and then filled again.

If something here feels familiar in a way you cannot explain, that is not coincidence. It means we share a particular kind of wildness.

— 甜慕, somewhere between midnight and morning ✿

🔐 How Admin Access Works

There is no visible admin button on the site. To open the admin panel, click the site logo 5 times quickly.

Your password is stored as a SHA-256 hash — not in plain text. Even if someone reads the source code they cannot reverse the hash to find your password.

After 3 wrong attempts, the login locks for 60 seconds. Any changes made by others only affect their own browser — your real files on GitHub stay untouched.

📝 How to Use Footnotes

In any chapter content, add footnote references using [^1] inline, then define them at the end of the content: 

<p>She walked into the garden.[^1] The roses were red.[^2]</p>

[^1]: The garden was described earlier in chapter 2.
[^2]: Red roses symbolise passion in this story.

Footnotes appear as coral superscripts — tap/click one to see a popup. They're also listed at the bottom of the chapter.
